Mission

We equip teenagers with the tools to build healthy relationships in a digital and interconnected Europe. Through international experiences and interactive workshops, we create safe spaces where young people learn about sexuality, relational intelligence and equality, to prevent sexual, gender-based and cyber violence.

Vision

We envision a Europe where relational and sexual learning is valued as essential as academic education. Because the next generation deserves more than silence, confusion, or algorithms teaching them about relationships. They deserve education, dialogue, and better role models.

Values

Curiosity | Inclusivity | Safety | Empathy | Non-violent communication
